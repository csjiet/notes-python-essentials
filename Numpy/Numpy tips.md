> **Foreword**: Presents ways to manipulate arrays in numpy format. Numpy arrays are often more compact, while their utility functions are also more efficient (using parallelism) . So it would be good to learn them.

Please read: [Absolute basic numpy operations](https://numpy.org/doc/stable/user/absolute_beginners.html#more-useful-array-operations)


### Indexing Routines:
- `numpy.c_[] && numpy.r_[]` - function that takes two arrays and outputs in a specific format.
